Meet the Artist

With over 12 years of experience in the art of tattooing, our artist has developed a unique style that blends traditional techniques with contemporary designs. Specializing in both color and black & grey work, they have mastered various tattoo styles including realism, neo-traditional, and Japanese art. Their passion for creating custom, meaningful pieces has earned them recognition in the industry and a loyal client base. Having completed advanced training in hygiene and safety protocols, they ensure every session meets the highest standards of professionalism and care.
